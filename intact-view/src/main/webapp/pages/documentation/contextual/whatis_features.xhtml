Representation of Experimental Features

This section shows the graphical representation of experimental features, where each participant is represented as a white rectangle with a black border and with it's features attached. All categories for the different feature types are represented in the right side of the legend. The left side of the legend dynamically shows the range statuses occuring in the shown interaction.
