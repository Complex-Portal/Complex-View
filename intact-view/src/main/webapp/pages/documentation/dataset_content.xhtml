Curated Datasets

Curated datasets are publications tagged, either computationally or manually by a curator, which are relevant to a specific area of biology. These are actively maintained andÂ grow with every release.
New datasets can be requested, if relevant to your work, by mailing intact-help@ebi.ac.uk.

Manually selected datasets

Cancer - Interactions investigated in the context of cancer AFCS - Interactions from the Alliance for Cell Signaling database BioCreative - Critical Assessment of Information Extraction systems in Biology Alzheimers Interaction - dataset based on proteins with an association to Alzheimer disease Complexes - Interaction dataset based on curated protein complexes. Chromatin - Epigenetic interactions resulting in chromatin modulation

Computationally maintained datasets

These datasets are computationally maintained but additional papers may be manually added to this set by a curator during the curation process. When datasets are computationally added to a publication, the large scale papers (more than 100 interactions per experiment) are excluded. Archaea - Interaction dataset based on Archaea proteins NDPK - Interactions involving proteins containing InterPro domain IPR001564, Nucleoside diphosphate kinase, core. MSD Data - obtained from The Macromolecular Structure Database Cyanobacteria - Interaction dataset based on Cyanobacteria proteins and related species Synapse - Interactions of proteins with an established role in the presynapse. Apoptosis - Interactions involving proteins with a function related to apoptosis
