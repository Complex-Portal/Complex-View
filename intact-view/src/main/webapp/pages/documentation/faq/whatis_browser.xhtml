This tab is meant to give you access to more content based on the currently selected set of interaction. As you can see, in this tab we have grouped features under the interacting molecule types. Please note that some of these functionalities will only allow you to include up to 200 molecules , if you exceed this number you will see the warning icon . Now let’s look at the features available to you: Clicking on the ‘List All’ present under each molecule type will open the corresponding list of molecule in the List Tab. Allows users to browse the GO hierarchy as a tree and select terms in order to narrow down their dataset. Once a term is selected, you are taken back to the interaction tab to review your dataset. Allows users to browse the ChEBI hierarchy as a tree and select terms in order to narrow down their dataset. Once a term is selected, you are taken back to the interaction tab to review your dataset.
  • Proteins by InterPro domain: Opens the InterPro domain search and shows in a single display the proteins interacting in your interaction set.
  • Proteins by Reactome pathway: Sends your proteins to the Reactome SkyPainter that will show you the pathways in which these molecules are know to play a role.
  • Proteins by Chromosomal location: Sends your list of proteins to Ensembl’s Karyotype viewer and overlays the proteins on the chromosomes.
  • Proteins by mRNA expression: Sends your set of proteins to the ArrayExpress Atlas that will show the known gene expression based on experimental studies.
