Can I add terms to the controlled vocabulary?

Two different types of controlled vocabulary are used; PSI-MI and GO. GO describes the biological-process, cellular-component and molecular-function of a protein. The PSI-MI describes the types of interactions between the proteins and the different experiemtnal methods by which they were discovered.

To propose new terms in the PSI-MI controlled vocaulary either email psidev-mi-dev@lists.sourceforge.net or visit the PSI-MI Sourceforge website and use the "MI controlled vocab changes" Tracker to sublit your query. Sourceforge is a web-environemnt for managing open-source software projects using Trackers to provide an archive of queries/requests submitted along with their responses and a library of software released. Note: to submit new term on the website you will need to have a Sourceforge account and be logged-in.

GO also has a presense on the Sourceforge website and new terms can be proposed by using the "Curator requests" Tracker. GO also has a thorough website at http://www.geneontology.org/.

Please send any questions or suggestions to intact-help@ebi.ac.uk
